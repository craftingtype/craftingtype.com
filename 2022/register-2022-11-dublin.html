Group Discounts
We offer group discounts of 20% from our full prices for 3+ people. Email info@craftingtype.com to find out more!

Font Editing Software

Attendees are welcome to use any font editing application they wish. At this event, we can best support FontLab 8 (or 6/7). We can offer much help for FontForge and Glyphs, but it won’t always be tool-specific..


Fontlab

Crafting Type is happy to welcome FontLab as a sponsor. FontLab offers:

  • access to a fully-functional version of any FontLab font editor for 30 days. FontLab VI recommended.
  • 30% Discounts for all Crafting Type attendees on both regular and academic pricing (for both students and teachers) of FontLab (so yes, that's a total of over 75% off for academic pricing!)

Glyphs

Crafting Type is happy to welcome Glyphs as a sponsor. Glyphs offers:

  • access to a fully-functional version of Glyphs for 30 days.
  • 10% discount for all attendees on regular and academic pricing of Glyphs

TruFont

Crafting Type is happy to welcome TruFont as a sponsor. TruFont offers:

  • basic features, suitable for beginners, and a modern scripting environment
  • access to a fully-functional version forever, for free
  • complete access to the development process on Github

FontForge

Crafting Type is happy to welcome FontForge as a sponsor. FontForge offers:

  • many features, although not as advanced as other restricted-license editors
  • access to a fully-functional version forever, for free
  • complete access to the development process on Github
