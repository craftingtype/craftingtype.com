Font Editing Software

Attendees are welcome to use any font editing application they wish. At this particular event, Thomas can best support FontLab VI, FontLab Studio, and TypeTool, and will do his best at FontForge, Glyphs, and/or Fontographer if needed.

FontLab

Crafting Type is happy to welcome FontLab as a sponsor (FontLab CEO Thomas Phinney is a Crafting Type instructor). FontLab offers:

  • access to fully-functional versions of FontLab Studio 5 and/or FontLab VI (download VI now) for at least one month.
  • 50% discounts for all attendees on regular and academic pricing of FontLab Studio 5 (includes a free future upgrade to FontLab VI)

FontForge

Crafting Type is happy to welcome FontForge as a sponsor. FontForge offers:

  • access to a fully-functional version of FontForge forever, for free
  • complete access to the development process on Github

You can directly improve FontForge by giving the project developers a tip. We work with freelance programmer Frank Trampe to fix issues that matter most to workshop participants. If you are in the USA, donations to TUG are fully tax deductible.
